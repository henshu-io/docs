After [generating your episode](/publishing/generating), you can download the final audio file and share it with your audience.

## Overview

Once generation is complete, your finished podcast episode is available as a downloadable audio file. You can then upload it to your podcast hosting platform, share it directly, or use it however you like.

## How do I download my generated episode?

After generation completes, click **Download** in the Export dialog. You can choose between two formats:

- **WAV** is the raw, uncompressed file. Best for archiving or further editing in another tool.
- **M4A** is a compressed file. Smaller file size, suitable for uploading to podcast hosts.

[screenshot: Download button on a generated episode]

## What can I do with the file?

Once downloaded, you can:

- Upload to a podcast host (Spotify for Podcasters, Apple Podcasts Connect, Buzzsprout, Podbean, Anchor, etc.)
- Share directly by sending the file to collaborators or posting it on social media
- Archive a local copy of your finished episodes

Henshu handles the editing and production. Distribution is up to you and your podcast host.

## Key Details

- Your generated file stays available for download until you generate a new version, which replaces it.
- WAV and M4A formats are available for each generation
- Henshu handles editing and production. Distribution is up to you and your podcast host. We're working on direct publishing to hosting platforms so you can go from editing to live in one step.

## Common Issues

**Download link isn't working**
Try refreshing the page. If the download still doesn't start, the generated file may still be processing. Wait for the episode status to show **Generated** before attempting to download.

**I need a different file format**
Henshu offers WAV and M4A downloads. If you need a different format for your hosting platform, use a free audio converter tool after downloading.
