Manage your Henshu subscription: upgrade to Standard, switch between monthly and yearly billing, or cancel.

## How do I upgrade?

1. Click your profile icon and go to **Subscription** in Settings
2. Choose **Monthly** or **Yearly** billing
3. Click **Upgrade now** on the Standard plan
4. Enter your payment details on the Stripe checkout page
5. You're redirected back to Henshu with your plan active immediately

[screenshot: Subscription settings showing Free and Standard plans]

## What does Standard include?

Everything in Free, plus:

- Premium BGM library
- Unlimited storage (keep all episodes)
- Unlimited transcription
- Priority AI processing

See [Plans & Quotas](/account/plans-and-quotas) for full details on limits.

## How do I manage my billing?

Once subscribed, a **Manage Subscription** option appears in your Subscription settings. This opens the Stripe customer portal where you can:

- Update your credit card or payment method
- View and download past invoices
- Switch between monthly and yearly billing
- See your next billing date

## How do I cancel?

1. Go to **Subscription** in Settings
2. Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Stripe portal
3. Cancel from there

Your Standard plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan. All your shows, episodes, and content remain intact. You can re-subscribe at any time.

## FAQ

**Is my payment information secure?**
Yes. Henshu uses Stripe for payment processing. Your card details are handled entirely by Stripe and never touch Henshu's servers.

**Will I lose my data if I cancel?**
No. Everything stays. You revert to the Free plan with its quota limits.
