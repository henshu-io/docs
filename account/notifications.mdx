Henshu sends email notifications when background tasks finish, like audio enhancement or episode generation. You can control which notifications you receive.

## What notifications does Henshu send?

- Audio enhancement completed
- Episode generation completed

## How do I change my notification settings?

1. Go to your account settings
2. Find the **Notifications** section
3. Toggle individual notification types on or off
